About Me

안녕하세요, 한양대학교 나노광전자학과 구윤모입니다.
저는 Through-Glass Via(TGV) 기반 마이크로LED 패키징, SU-8 고종횡비 구조 제작, Soldering 그리고 Micro LED와 Fluidic Self-Assembly(FSA)를 이용한 대면적 고정밀 전사 기술을 연구하고 있습니다.

Research Interest

Topic 01
TGV Metallization & Solder Interface Metallization of TGV substrates and stabilization of solder-electrode interfaces for reliable packaging.
Topic 02
Micro-structure Design for FSA Chip alignment and capture structure design using SU-8 microstructures for precise assembly.
Topic 03
Micro LED Transfer Process Optimization of Fluidic Self-Assembly (FSA) based Micro LED transfer processes for large-area displays.
Topic 04
Optical Alignment System Development of MEMS mirror-based optical alignment systems for advanced optoelectronic integration.
