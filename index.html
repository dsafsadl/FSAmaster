Research Themes:

Smart Scalable Systems (3S)

His research interests include Through-Glass Via (TGV) based Micro LED packaging, SU-8 high-aspect-ratio structure fabrication, and advanced transfer technologies using Fluidic Self-Assembly (FSA).
  • We fabricate Intelligent micro devices by semiconductor technology.
  • By using self-assembly technology, we assemble micro devices for many different applications including Stretchable and Flexible electronics.

Research Interest

Topic 01
TGV Metallization & Solder Interface

Metallization of TGV substrates and stabilization of solder-electrode interfaces for reliable packaging.

Topic 02
Micro-structure Design for FSA

Chip alignment and capture structure design using SU-8 microstructures for precise assembly.

Topic 03
Micro LED Transfer Process

Optimization of Fluidic Self-Assembly (FSA) based Micro LED transfer processes for large-area displays.

Topic 04
Optical Alignment System

Development of MEMS mirror-based optical alignment systems for advanced optoelectronic integration.
