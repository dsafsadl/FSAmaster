Detailed Research Areas

TGV Metallization & Interface

We are developing advanced metallization techniques for Through-Glass Via (TGV) substrates. This research focuses on optimizing the electrical and mechanical properties of the via filling process.

  • High-quality Cu filling for TGV
  • Solder-electrode interface stabilization
  • Reliability testing for advanced packaging

Micro-structure Design for FSA

Precise alignment of micro-components is crucial for next-generation displays. We design and fabricate SU-8 microstructures to capture and align chips with high accuracy.

  • SU-8 High-aspect-ratio structures
  • Fluidic Self-Assembly (FSA) receptor design
  • Hydrodynamic simulation and optimization

Micro LED Transfer Process

To realize large-area Micro LED displays, we are optimizing the Fluidic Self-Assembly (FSA) process. This technology enables massive parallel assembly of LED chips.

  • High-yield transfer technology
  • Defect repair mechanisms
  • Large-area substrate integration

Optical Alignment System

Development of MEMS mirror-based optical alignment systems for precise integration of optoelectronic devices.

  • MEMS mirror control
  • Active optical alignment
  • Automated assembly systems
